From Healthcare to Tech_nology

Helping others has always been one of my main motivators and while I enjoyed this part of my current role as a Healthcare Assistant these past two years, I ultimately felt unfulfilled and restless. The role didn’t stimulate my mind and restricted any kind of creativity.

Naturally being tech-savvy, I began coding in my spare time; not only to learn a new skill but also to provide an outlet for the stress acquired on the job. I quickly learnt that programming appealed to me immensely. From the problem-solving nature to the almost limitless possibilities it provides as a career; the option to work in almost any environment in any field of work I find interesting, thereby I can produce something I find meaningful and helps make someone’s life easier.

Another aspect I find hugely appealing is that it allows you to be both logical and creative. I have always had an appreciation for great design and architecture, something which I think translates brilliantly to code. An application needs to be coded (structured) efficiently; able to achieve its intended purpose with as little code (resources) as possible. It also needs to look the part, designed with its environment in mind; intuitive and effortless to use.

The continual learning, blend of logic and creativity and the almost limitless possibilities a career in development brings are why I have decided to join the course at _nology.

I believe it will provide an excellent foundation on which to build my skills as well provide a collaborative learning environment in which we are all working towards achieving a common goal. Upon completion, I hope to go down the path of front-end development and possibly UX design in the future.
